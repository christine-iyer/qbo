Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 12, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Invoice Me ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal and business information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account credentials and authentication information
  • QuickBooks Online authorization and access tokens
  • Business information (company name, contact details)
  • Customer information you create or import
  • Invoice data and transaction details

2.2 Information from QuickBooks

  • Customer lists and contact information
  • Product and service items
  • Invoice and payment records
  • Company settings and preferences

2.3 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage data and application interactions
  • IP address and device information
  • Browser type and version
  • Access times and dates

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our invoicing services
  • Process and manage invoices through QuickBooks Online
  • Sync customer and item data between our application and QuickBooks
  • Generate delivery reports and manage destinations
  • Send email notifications related to invoices and deliveries
  • Respond to your requests and provide customer support
  • Detect, prevent, and address technical issues
  • Analyze usage patterns to improve user experience

4. QuickBooks Integration

Our application integrates with QuickBooks Online using their official API. When you authorize our application:

  • We request only the permissions necessary to provide our services
  • We use OAuth 2.0 for secure authentication
  • Your QuickBooks credentials are never stored by our application
  • Access tokens are securely encrypted and stored
  • You can revoke our access at any time through your QuickBooks account settings

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

  • All data transmission is encrypted using SSL/TLS protocols
  • Access tokens and sensitive data are encrypted at rest
  • Regular security assessments and updates
  • Limited employee access to personal information
  • Secure server infrastructure with monitoring

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.
